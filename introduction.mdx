## What is Revocalize AI?
Revocalize trains humanlike AI voice models for voice-to-voice conversion and for text-to-singing & speech.

We've developed innovative AI voice synthesizing technology, producing humanlike results even with very limited training data.

Revocalize is used in a variety of creative applications, including music production, advertising, conversational AI, translation & dubbing, podcasts and social media content.

## How does it work?

Revocalize AI uses a deep learning technique called [Generative Adversarial Networks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Generative_adversarial_network) (GANs) to train studio-quality AI voice models.

## What can I do with Revocalize AI?

Revocalize AI can be used to create ultra-realistic AI voices for a variety of applications, including:

- **Music Production** - Create music with AI voices.
- **Advertising** - Create advertising content with AI voices.
- **Social Media Content** - Create social media content with AI voices.
- **Conversational AI** - Create conversational AI with AI voices.
- **Podcasts** - Create podcasts with AI voices.
- **Voice Cloning** - Clone your own voice.
- **Voice Conversion** - Convert one voice to another.
- **Text-to-Speech** - Convert text to humanlike speech.
- **Text-to-Singing** - Convert text to singing.
- **Singing Voice Conversion** - Convert one singing voice to another.
- **Voice Synthesis** - Synthesize your voice to sound like a celebrity.
- **Voice Translation** - Translate your voice to another language.
- **Voice Dubbing** - Dub your voice to another language.
- **Voice Effects** - Add effects to your voice.
- **Voiceover** - Create voiceovers for your videos.
